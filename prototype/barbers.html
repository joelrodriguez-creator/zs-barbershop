ZIAD DIB

Ziad opened the shop on Bird Road over a decade ago after learning the trade in Lebanon from his father. He specializes in old-school men's cuts and the traditional Lebanese hot-towel shave — the way it's done in the old country, with steam, straight razor, and time. Cubans, Lebanese, anyone who needs a fade and a story — Ziad's chair is open.
