ZIAD DIB

Ziad opened the shop on Bird Road over a decade ago after learning the trade in Lebanon from his father. He specializes in old-school men's cuts, the way it's done in the old country, with patience, conversation, and a real chair. Regulars joke he's the real-life Zohan from the Adam Sandler movie, the Middle Eastern barber with scissors in hand, except Ziad's cuts actually work. Cubans, Lebanese, anyone who needs a fade and a story: Ziad's chair is open.
