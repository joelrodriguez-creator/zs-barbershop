Old-School Barbering

Ziad Dib learned to cut hair in Lebanon, where the hot-towel shave isn't a special service — it's how every barber works. He brought the old-school tradition to Westchester, and ten years later, he's still working out of the same chair on Bird Road.

Cubans, Lebanese, anyone who needs a fade and a story — the door is open.
